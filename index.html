Why should people be healthy? Well, it can benefit in a lot of things. For example, health. It can hugely benefit for a lot of diseases, like heart attacks. Heart attacks work like this: When you eat something unhealthy like a burger, all the fat gets stored on the sides of the veins. What does this mean? Well, it means that the more fat you eat, the more fat is stored on the sides of the veins, causing the heart to pump harder and faster to reach all the parts of the body. So, as a result, the heart may fail and that is what causes heart attacks.
